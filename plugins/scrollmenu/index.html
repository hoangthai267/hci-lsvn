jQuery Scroll Up Menu Plugin Demo

The header (and dummy menu) above is fixed, but gets out of the way after a little scrolling. If you then scroll up, the menu rolls down at the top of the page. This is really useful for mobile browsers with little space for annoying fixed headers.

Usage: 

$('#top').scrollUpMenu();
